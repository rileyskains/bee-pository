What do you know about bees?

Of course you know that bees like flowers, some make honey, and some have the potential to sting you, but what do you really know about bees?

The world of bees is far more complex than you realize! With complex social structures, a huge impact on the environment, and a history that dates back to the dawn of mankind, bees really make the world go round.

To start with, there are three different types of bees in every hive, and each one has quite a bit to do every day.
